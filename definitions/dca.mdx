**DCA**: Dollar-cost averaging. DCA is an investment strategy in which an 
you divide up the total amount to be invested across periodic purchases. 
You do this to reduce the impact of volatility on the overall purchase. 
The purchases occur regardless of the asset's price and at regular intervals.
